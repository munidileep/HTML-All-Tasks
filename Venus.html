Venus

Introduction

Venus is the second planet from the Sun, and the sixth largest planet. It’s the hottest planet in our solar system.Venus is a cloud-swaddled planet named for a love goddess, and often called Earth’s twin. But pull up a bit closer, and Venus turns hellish. Our nearest planetary neighbor, the second planet from the Sun, has a surface hot enough to melt lead. The atmosphere is so thick that, from the surface, the Sun is just a smear of light.Venus is similar in structure and size to Earth, and is sometimes called Earth's evil twin. Its thick atmosphere traps heat in a runaway greenhouse effect.Venus is named for the ancient Roman goddess of love and beauty, who was known as Aphrodite to the ancient Greeks.
